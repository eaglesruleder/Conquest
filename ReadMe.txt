//-----------------------------------//
	Conquest: Frontier Wars
//-----------------------------------//

A stand-alone executable can be found at /Builds
Note: This requires Modify security settings for its directory
	- If you do not know how to apply these, right click the executable and hit "Run as Administrator"

- Input
Left click to select
Left click and drag to select units in an area

Right click on an empty space to move
	- Click and drag to move to the location, and change the height of the unit

Right click on a unit to attack
	- Alt-Right click to attack a friendly unit
